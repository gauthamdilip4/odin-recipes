Lasagna

Description

A classic Italian baked pasta dish made with layers of pasta sheets, rich meat sauce, and creamy cheese.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Brown the ground beef in a pan, then mix in the marinara sauce.
  3. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and pepper.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella in a baking dish. Repeat layers.
  5. Top with parmesan cheese and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 45 minutes.
  6. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
