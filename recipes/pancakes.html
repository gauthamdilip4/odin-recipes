Pancakes

Description

Light and fluffy pancakes, perfect for a quick and satisfying breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3.5 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1.25 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp melted butter

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  4. Heat a lightly greased griddle or pan over medium heat.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup of batter per pancake and cook until bubbles form, then flip.
  6. Cook the other side until golden brown. Serve warm with syrup.
